# What actually matters in coding interviews

- Clear understanding of the problem
- Structured approach (brute force → optimize)
- Explaining your thinking out loud
- Choosing the right pattern
- Writing clean and readable code
- Handling edge cases properly
- Testing your solution with examples
- Staying calm under pressure

> You don't need the fastest solution. You need clarity and structure.
